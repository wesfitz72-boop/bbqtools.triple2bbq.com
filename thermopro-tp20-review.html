🔍 ThermoPro TP20 Review

Best for: Smoking brisket, pork butts, ribs — or anything that requires long cooks with exact temps.

What We Love

  • Dual-probe wireless temp tracking (meat + chamber)
  • Accurate within ±1.8°F
  • 300ft wireless range
  • No app or phone required — standalone receiver

What Could Be Better

  • Probes aren't dishwasher safe
  • Some users report screen fades after 1–2 years

Our Verdict

We've run the TP20 on our XLR350 and offset pits for years. It's one of the most trusted tools in the backyard game. Not fancy — just reliable.
